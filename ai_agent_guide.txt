  SECTION 1 — GAME OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

You are a nasal spray dealer in a world where nasal spray is illegal.
The goal is to make money by buying spray, setting prices, selling to customers,
leveling up, and surviving mafia loans and police inspections.

Core loop:
  1. Buy spray stock  (--buy)
  2. Set your sell prices  (--set-price)
  3. Sell to customers  (--sell or --serve)
  4. Level up when you have enough money  (--upgrade)
  5. Expand customer base and unlock better stock at higher levels

There is no "turn" system. Game state is real-time: the mafia loan deadline
is a real clock, and in-game days pass based on real elapsed time.

  Joseph  — Better at selling to female customers (+30% charisma).
             Slightly worse vs stingy customers (-5%).

             Best for charm-based long-term customer building.

After --init you start with:
  Balance:   €50.00
  Inventory: 2x normal spray
  Level:     1

  SECTION 3 — READING GAME STATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --status
    Full snapshot: balance, level, inventory, loan, customers, day/time, etc.

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --inventory
    Focused view: what you have, quantities, base prices, your set prices.

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --customers
    All available customers with their WTP (willingness to pay, 1–10),
    gender, and whether they are won/regular/winnable.

Always run --status at the start of any session to orient yourself.

Key fields in --status output:
  balance              Your current money in euros
  level                1, 2, or 3 — gates what you can buy and do
  inventory            List of items in hand (what you can sell right now)
  hidden_stash         Items auto-hidden during a police inspection
  pending_samples      Winnable customers you've sent samples to
  won_customers        Customers you've permanently unlocked
  unlocked_customers   Base regular customer pool (always available)
  loan                 null if no debt, or {amount, seconds_remaining}
  under_inspection     true if police are currently searching you
  inspection_ends_in   Seconds until inspection ends (null if not under one)
  police_skip_charges  Charges of "Korrupter Polizist" perk remaining

Higher-tier sprays have a higher base price and therefore can be sold at a

Buying ultra at level 3 is the most profitable per unit.

If you try to buy premium at level 1 or ultra at level 1/2, you get:
  {"ok": false, "error": "Premium Nasenspray requires level 2."}

  SECTION 5 — PRICING (--set-price)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --set-price TYPE --price VALUE

Sets your permanent sell price for a spray type. Persists across sessions.

The sell chance formula:
  - If your price ≤ base_price × (1 + tolerance), chance = 95%
  - tolerance = 0.05 + (customer_WTP / 10) × 0.5
  - Above the threshold, chance drops by 1.5% per % over the threshold
  - Minimum chance: 5%. Maximum: 97% (after character bonuses).

In practice:
  - A WTP-9 customer tolerates up to ~55% markup before chance falls below 95%
  - A WTP-2 customer tolerates up to ~15% markup
  - A WTP-5 customer tolerates up to ~30% markup

The --set-price command returns sell_chances_by_wtp for WTP 2, 5, and 9 so
you can evaluate your price before committing.

Good starting prices (safe for most customers):
  normal:   €16–€18   (base €15, ~7–20% markup)
  menthol:  €24–€26   (base €22, ~9–18% markup)
  premium:  €38–€42   (base €35, ~9–20% markup)
  ultra:    €80–€90   (base €75, ~7–20% markup)

For low-WTP customers (Stefan, Johnathan, Ralf, etc.) you can use
--special-price on a per-sale basis without changing your global price.

  SECTION 6 — SELLING TO CUSTOMERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TWO WAYS TO SELL:

─── A) --serve (random ticks) ───────────────────────────────────────────────

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --serve --ticks N

Simulates N game ticks. Each tick resolves one event:
  - A random customer visits and a sell attempt is made automatically
    using your set prices (the first item in inventory is offered)
  - OR a police check triggers if the timer is up

Returns a list of events with results. Good for "just keep the game running."
Use --ticks 10 or higher to process a burst of activity.

If inventory is empty, customer visits result in "no_inventory" events (no sale).
Always buy stock before serving.

─── B) --sell --customer NAME (targeted) ────────────────────────────────────

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --sell --customer NAME
  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --sell --customer NAME --special-price 12.0

Directly attempt to sell to a specific customer. Uses the first item
in inventory at your set price (or --special-price if given).

Use --special-price for:
  - Low-WTP customers (WTP < 4) where your global price would rarely sell
  - Negotiating a one-off discount without permanently lowering your price
  - Triggering the WTP-raise mechanic (if you use a special price AND the
    sale succeeds, there's a character-based chance the customer's WTP goes up
    permanently for future sales)

Customer must be in unlocked_customers or won_customers (from --status).
You cannot sell to winnable customers until you win them via --contact-customer.

  SECTION 9 — LEVELING UP (--upgrade)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --upgrade

Levels: 1 → 2 → 3 (max). Requires money. Cost scales with level and drops
if your average customer rating is high (high ratings = cheaper upgrades).
Having done a rebirth also reduces upgrade cost by 30%.

Approximate costs (no rebirth, average rating ~3.0):
  Level 1 → 2:  ~€133
  Level 2 → 3:  ~€503

What each level unlocks:
  Level 2:
    - --buy premium (€35/unit, higher markup potential)
    - --contact-customer (winnable customer system)
    - Phone system (cosmetic, no mechanical effect in headless mode)

  Level 3:
    - --buy ultra (€75/unit, best margins)
    - --buy-distraction (police interval upgrades)

After upgrading, always run --status to confirm your new level.

  SECTION 11 — REBIRTH SYSTEM (--rebirth + --rebirth-perk)

REBIRTH (prestige reset):
  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --rebirth

Requirements: Must be at level 3.
Effect: Resets level to 1, balance to €50, inventory to 2x normal.
        Awards +3 rebirth points (RP).
        Rebirth count increases by 1 (future upgrade costs drop 30%).

Only do this when you've maxed level 3 and want the RP bonuses.
Your won customers and customer ratings are preserved.

  SECTION 12 — MAFIA LOAN SYSTEM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BORROW:
  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --mafia-borrow --amount 50

  Max loan: €100. Only one loan at a time.
  Deadline: 7 in-game days = 7 × day_length_seconds real time.
  Default day length = 1200s, so deadline = 8400 seconds (~2.3 real hours).
  If you don't repay in time: GAME OVER, save is wiped.

FULL REPAY:
  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --mafia-repay

  Pays off the entire loan at once. Requires sufficient balance.

PARTIAL REPAY:
  python3 dummtopia_headless.py --mafia-repay-partial --amount 25

  Reduces loan by the given amount. Loan deadline stays the same.
  Use this to chip away at debt while keeping cash for stock.

CHECK LOAN STATUS:
  Run --status and look at the "loan" field:
    "loan": {"amount": 50.0, "seconds_remaining": 7204}

  If seconds_remaining < 600 (10 min), prioritize repayment immediately.
  If seconds_remaining reaches 0 before repayment: save file is deleted.

STRATEGY:
  Only borrow if you need capital to buy stock for an immediate profit play.
  Borrow €50–€80, buy stock, sell quickly, repay before deadline.
  Never borrow if you already have an active loan.

  SECTION 13 — POLICE INSPECTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Police arrive on a timer (default every 60–120 seconds of real time).
When triggered during --serve, the game auto-hides your inventory to the stash.

What happens during an inspection:
  - If inventory is empty: quick check (5–15s), nothing found, no penalty.
  - If inventory exists + "Korrupter Polizist" charge available:
      charge consumed, no penalty, 10–20s inspection.
  - Otherwise: ALL inventory auto-moved to hidden_stash. Inspection lasts 30–60s.
      During inspection, no sales are possible.

After inspection ends (on the next --serve tick):
  - Stash is automatically returned to inventory.
  - You can sell again.

In --status, check:
  "under_inspection": true/false
  "inspection_ends_in": seconds remaining (null if not under inspection)

If under_inspection is true, --sell will return an error. Wait for it to end
by running --serve once or twice to process the end-of-inspection tick.

NOTE: In headless mode there is no "bust" from auto-hide because the game
hides everything instantly. You can only be busted in the original interactive
mode where you fail the QTE. Headless is therefore safer than the original.

  SECTION 14 — COMPLETE COMMAND REFERENCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

── SETUP ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --init
    Required before first use. Creates a new save file with default state.
    Optional: --character [Philipp|Joseph]  (default: Philipp)
    Optional: --name NAME                   (default: same as character)
    Example:  --init --character Joseph --name DealerJoe

  --reset
    Deletes the save file entirely. Use if you want a full restart.
    Does NOT require --init to have been run first.

── INFORMATION ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --status
    Full JSON state snapshot. Run this first every session.

  --inventory
    Current inventory and stash with prices. Shorter than --status.

  --customers
    All customers: WTP, gender, won/pending/available status.

── STOCK ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --buy TYPE --qty N
    Buy N units of TYPE spray. Deducted from balance immediately.
    Types: normal (€15) | menthol (€22) | premium (€35, lv2) | ultra (€75, lv3)
    Example: --buy normal --qty 5

  --set-price TYPE --price VALUE
    Set your sell price for a spray type. Persists across sessions.
    Returns sell chance preview for WTP 2, 5, and 9.
    Example: --set-price normal --price 18.0

── SELLING ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --serve
    Simulate one random game tick (customer visit or police check).
    Optional: --ticks N  to run N ticks at once (default 1).
    Example: --serve --ticks 10

  --sell --customer NAME
    Directly sell to a named customer using your set price.
    Optional: --special-price VALUE  to override price for this sale only.
    Customer must be in unlocked_customers or won_customers.
    Example: --sell --customer Stefan
    Example: --sell --customer Ralf --special-price 14.0

── CUSTOMER EXPANSION ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --contact-customer NAME
    Pay to send a sample to a winnable customer. Requires level 2.
    Cost = €20 + (WTP × 5). Customer goes into pending_samples.
    Example: --contact-customer Stefanie  (costs €65)

  --deliver-sample NAME
    Deliver a pending sample to roll for winning the customer.
    Can also use --sell --customer NAME if they appear in a --serve tick.
    Example: --deliver-sample Stefanie

── PROGRESSION ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --upgrade
    Pay to advance from current level to next (1→2 or 2→3).
    Cost depends on your level and average customer rating.
    Fails with error if insufficient balance.

  --rebirth
    Requires level 3. Resets to level 1 + €50 + 2x normal in exchange for
    +3 rebirth points. Unlock cost reduced 30% on future runs.

  --rebirth-perk "PERK NAME"
    Spend rebirth points. Perk name must be exact (use quotes):
      "Goldene Nase"          1 RP  — prices -10%
      "VIP-Liste"             2 RP  — +2 WTP all customers
      "Korrupter Polizist"    3 RP  — 1x police skip charge
      "Schwarzmarkt-Kontakt"  5 RP  — instant level 3

  --buy-distraction "ITEM NAME"
    Requires level 3. Increases police check intervals.
      "Dummy-Kiosk"       €150  — +30s min interval
      "Fake-Marktstand"   €300  — +60s max interval
      "Bestechung"        €500  — +120s both intervals

── MAFIA ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  --mafia-borrow --amount N
    Borrow up to €100 from the mafia. Must be repaid within ~2.3 real hours.
    Game over (save wiped) if deadline passes unpaid.
    Example: --mafia-borrow --amount 80

  --mafia-repay
    Repay the entire active loan. Requires enough balance.

  --mafia-repay-partial --amount N
    Repay part of the loan. Deadline does not extend.
    Example: --mafia-repay-partial --amount 30

  SECTION 15 — RECOMMENDED AGENT STRATEGY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EARLY GAME (Level 1):
  1. --init --character Philipp  (or Joseph if you prefer female customers)
  2. --set-price normal --price 17  (safe markup, ~95% on most customers)
  3. --set-price menthol --price 25
  4. --buy normal --qty 5
  5. --serve --ticks 20
  6. Repeat steps 4–5 until balance > €150
  7. --upgrade  (to level 2)

MID GAME (Level 2):
  1. --buy premium --qty 3
  2. --set-price premium --price 40
  3. --contact-customer Stefanie  (best value winnable customer)
  4. --serve --ticks 10 until Stefanie appears, then --deliver-sample Stefanie
  5. Accumulate ~€550 for level 3 upgrade
  6. --upgrade  (to level 3)

LATE GAME (Level 3):
  1. --buy ultra --qty 2
  2. --set-price ultra --price 88
  3. --buy-distraction "Bestechung"  (if balance allows)
  4. Win more high-WTP customers (Dietmar, Dr_Müller, Petra, Fatima)
  5. Grind to level 3 max, then --rebirth for RP
  6. --rebirth-perk "VIP-Liste"  (best RP spend)

LOAN PLAY (optional):
  If balance is low but you need stock:
  1. --mafia-borrow --amount 60
  2. --buy normal --qty 4
  3. --serve --ticks 15
  4. --mafia-repay
  Net profit if all 4 sell at €18: €72 revenue, €60 repaid = €12+ gain plus
  whatever you had before.

ALWAYS:
  - Check --status before every session to see loan countdown
  - Never let loan seconds_remaining drop below 600 without repaying
  - Keep at least one item in inventory or --serve ticks become wasteful
  - Prioritize upgrading over hoarding cash — each level unlocks better margins

  SECTION 16 — ERROR HANDLING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

All errors return:  {"ok": false, "error": "...", "details": {...}}

Common errors and how to fix them:

  "Game not initialized. Run --init first."
    → Run --init before any other command.

  "Not enough money. Need €X, have €Y."
    → Sell more before buying. Or use --mafia-borrow if desperate.

  "Premium Nasenspray requires level 2."
    → Run --upgrade first.

  "Customer 'X' not available."
    → Check --customers. They may need to be won via --contact-customer first.

  "No pending sample for 'X'."
    → Run --contact-customer X before --deliver-sample X.

  "Already have an active loan."
    → Repay with --mafia-repay before borrowing again.

  "Currently under police inspection."
    → Run --serve once to let the inspection expire, then retry --sell.

  "Not enough RP."
    → Do a --rebirth to earn more RP (requires level 3).

  "Unknown perk 'X'."
    → Use the exact perk name in quotes from Section 11. Case-sensitive.
